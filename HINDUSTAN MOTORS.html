CONTESSA

SPECIFICATIONS :

The Hindustan Contessa was a model of car which was manufactured by Hindustan Motors (HM) of India from 1984 to 2002.

It was based on the Vauxhall VX Series of 1976 to 1978, itself a development of the Vauxhall Victor FE.

When introduced in 1983, it was one of the few Indian manufactured luxury cars in the market.
